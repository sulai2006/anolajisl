About Us

WELCOME TO ANOLA INTEGRATED SERVICES LIMITED


Anola Integrated Services Limited is an entity specializing in the development and provision of renewable energy solutions and technology. Through collaborative efforts with our partners, we have significantly impacted the global renewable energy deployment industry. Our core business activities encompass the design, sales, installation, and maintenance of renewable energy solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients, spanning residential houses, commercial establishments, and communities.

Anola Integrated Services Limited is a multi-faceted company specializing in renewable energy services, power projects, project management, procurement solutions, and serving as importers' representatives and primary distributors of electrical and power products. Our reputation is built on the sale of authentic electrical and power materials from our renowned electrical shop/warehouse.

Our team is composed of seasoned professionals with substantial experience in power solutions, procurement, project management, marketing, risk assessment, and various other services. Collaborating with key players in the power sector and construction industry, we endeavor to bridge gaps along the value chain, optimizing costs while ensuring a consistent power supply for end-users and reducing business overhead expenses.

Our primary goal is to provide reliable and efficient energy services, unwavering in our commitment to quality and safety. We aim not only to showcase our skills and competitive pricing but also to establish enduring client relationships that extend well beyond the completion of our initial projects.

With a collective experience spanning over ninety years in both the public and private sectors, our team is well-equipped to deliver efficient services from project initiation to completion. At Hikima, our focus is on providing clients with practical, bespoke solutions that align with their strategic objectives.

Our Mission

To provide our clients with reliable and effective engineering solutions by strategically applying the necessary resources. Additionally, we are committed to delivering high-quality and dependable fixed engineering services, aiming to increase our customers' confidence in our capabilities.

Our Vision

To become a customer-focused organization of choice, offering quality, convenience and trust as the core of our business model. Bringing new technology, innovations and quality services to the satisfaction of all clients.
